================================================================================
COLLECTION SEQUENCES
================================================================================

FRESH (1-30 days overdue)
  6 actions across 30 days
  Tone: Friendly → Professional
  Goal: Payment reminder

AGING (31-60 days overdue)
  7 actions across 30 days
  Tone: Professional → Firm
  Goal: Payment demand

CRITICAL (61-90 days overdue)
  8 actions across 20 days
  Tone: Firm → Formal
  Goal: Escalation notice

SEVERE (90+ days overdue)
  6 actions across 10 days
  Tone: Formal → Final
  Goal: Pre-legal notice

================================================================================
INVOICE STATUSES
================================================================================

1. pending          - Not yet contacted
2. contacted        - At least one outreach attempt
3. promised         - Payment promised by debtor
4. partial_payment  - Some payment received
5. paid             - Fully paid
6. escalated        - Escalated to legal
7. written_off      - Uncollectible

================================================================================
TONE ESCALATION LEVELS
================================================================================

Level 1: Friendly
  - Courtesy reminder
  - Non-threatening
  - For 1-30 days overdue
  - Goal: Payment notice

Level 2: Professional
  - Direct, respectful
  - Clear payment request
  - For 31-60 days overdue
  - Goal: Payment prompt

Level 3: Firm
  - Assertive demand
  - Urgency emphasized
  - For 61-90 days overdue
  - Goal: Immediate action

Level 4: Formal
  - Legal language
  - Formal structure
  - For 90+ days overdue
  - Goal: Pre-legal notice

Level 5: Final
  - Ultimate demand
  - Legal action threatened
  - For 120+ days overdue
  - Goal: Last chance notice
